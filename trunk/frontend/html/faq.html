Frequently Asked Questions

What is All for Good? Why should I use it?

All for Good is a simple way for you to find and share volunteering opportunities with friends, family and colleagues. All for Good brings together listings from a number of service organizations and nonprofits to help you engage in service in a way that is meaningful to youon a national, regional or local leveland in a way that fits your individual volunteer capacity.

How does the site work?

Currently, you can go to All for Good and find volunteering opportunities that interest you. You can also use services like Facebook Connect to share opportunities with your friends and see what activities they are participating in. All for Good is still in an early development phase so new features will continue to be added in the next few months.

Do I need to sign in to use the site?

You do not need to sign in to search for volunteer opportunities. To share volunteer opportunities with your family/friends/colleagues or to see where your friends are volunteering, you will need to sign in. This can easily be done via your current Facebook Connect log in, as well as through your log in with Google, and Yahoo!.

Is All for Good free?

Yep!

Where do the postings come from?

Nonprofits, volunteer service listing providers and companies that help you find things and organize (like craigslist and MeetUp) provide their volunteer opportunities for use on the site.

Who can post volunteer opportunities?

Currently, only organized nonprofits, service and cause-related organizations can post listings for volunteer opportunities.

How does a group post a volunteer opportunity?

We welcome your listings and need your support! Help us expand our volunteer listings whether you have one opportunity or 1,000+ to share. We want to help you spread the word. If your organization is interested in submitting volunteer opportunities to All for Good, please contact us by filling out this form. We will get back to you with a choice of ways to deliver that data to us.

Can an individual post an opportunity?

Not at this time.

How do I volunteer for an opportunity?

To volunteer for an opportunity, simply search for opportunities of interest to you. There is no requirement to sign in to find opportunities. All for Good connects you with volunteer opportunities. Once you have found an opportunity of interest, you can sign up to volunteer with the organization sponsoring the opportunity.

How do I know the opportunities posted are legitimate? Is there an approval process before volunteer opportunities are accepted?

All for Good is working with established organizations to aggregate listings. All for Good has a strict Terms of Service that partners must follow when providing listings.

Can my nonprofit accept donations through All for Good? Can I donate to an organization through All for Good?

No. All for Good a simple way for you to find and share volunteering opportunities. The primary focus of All for Good is to help you donate your time and talent.

How are volunteer opportunities ranked on the page?

Individuals can search opportunities based on location and interest. Search results are based on relevance.

How can I find a listing that is no longer on your site?

You need to contact the organization who offered that volunteer opportunity directly.

Is All for Good available outside the U.S?

All for Good is primarily intended for use by U.S. audiences.

Does All for Good support nonprofit opportunities outside the U.S.? If so, how?

Currently, All for Good only lists volunteer opportunities within the U.S.

How do I notify Google of a copyright issue?

We comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and promptly remove content if we receive a formal copyright complaint as described below.

Before filing a complaint, we suggest you attempt to contact the individual who posted the copyrighted information on All for Good to request that he or she remove it. If this individual doesn't comply with your request, you can report a copyright issue by filing an official DMCA complaint.

To submit an official copyright complaint, you must own the copyright to the material in question or be authorized by the copyright holder to act on their behalf. You must clearly identify the material in question and provide us with the URL of the All for Good page on which it appears.

Can I post anonymously?

Currently, only organizations can provide listings. There is no option by an organization for an anonymous post at this time.

When will the product be final?

All for Good is being developed as an open source platform that allows the community-at-large, including individuals, to use, modify, and grow to ensure long-term relevance and utility. If you have suggestions for how to improve All for Good or some ideas on features you'd like to see, please let us know by sending us your feedback.

How will the site change from what I see on it today?

We are continuing to grow and scale the site to create and offer an optimal service experience. The goal is to have the community-at-large drive the growth and direction of the website. If you have suggestions for how to improve All for Good or some ideas for features you'd like to see, please let us know by emailing us your feedback .

Is All for Good a nonprofit?

All for Good was created by volunteers from companies, foundations and nonprofits that create a website to help connect more people with volunteer opportunities. The coalition is using the power of technology and the strength of community to meet President Obama's charge to get more people to serve. Although, All for Good is not-for-profit, we are not a 501(c) (3) organization at this time.
