Privacy Policy

May 2009

The All For Good website is hosted by Google, as such, for the purposes of personal information, the website operates as any other Google service.

At Google we recognize that privacy is important, the Google Privacy Policy describes how we treat personal information when you use Google's products and services, including information provided when you use All For Good. The Google Privacy Policy only applies to Google's hosting of the All For Good website.

The following describes our practices that are specific to All For Good. We hope this will help you make an informed decision about sharing personal information with us.

Personal Information

The following type of information is collected when you use the All For Good website ("Site") or the All For Good gadget:

* If you log into the Site using a third party social networking service such as Friend Connect or Facebook ("Social Service"), the Social Service sends Google a numeric ID corresponding to your user identification as well as authentication credentials. The authentication credentials are stored temporarily and will be deleted based on the expiry date set by the Social Service. Google stores the numeric ID sent by the Social Services, along with certain website activity that requires user identification (such as when you share an item, add an item or add an event to your calendar), in order to provide you with the ability to review your activities on the Site. You can delete the numeric ID and corresponding data from Google's servers by following the process outlined under "Your Choices" below. Residual copies of deleted data may take up to 60 days to be deleted from our active servers and may remain in our offline backup systems. No friend list information is collected by the Site.

  • Third party Social Services may use persistent cookies to authenticate sign-in and to remember users when they return to the Site. Google has no access to these cookies.
  • If you submit feedback or questions to the Site using a submission form, Google may use your email address for administrative purposes or to respond to you.
  • When you use the Site, Google's servers automatically record log information which may include information such as your IP address, the date and time of your visit, your browser and operating system type, and your referral URL. We use this data to provide the All For Good service to you.
  • Google may collect anonymous usage information about your use of the Site, such as which listings you click on or your search terms. This information is not associated with you and is only used by Google in aggregate.

Use of Information

Google uses this information to provide the All For Good service to you. We may also use anonymized, aggregate information for internal or administrative purposes. Such information does not identify you individually.

Information Sharing

  • You may share content from All For Good with your friends using Social Services such as Friend Connect or Facebook. You may choose to share your content with your friends anonymously.
  • The All For Good service is supported and maintained by several third party partners. These partners may have access to personal information you share with All For Good for the purposes of providing the All For Good service to you. Where partners may have access to your personal information, you will be given the opportunity to opt-out of that access at the time of collection. We require that these partners agree to process such information in compliance with this Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.
  • Google may share your anonymous, aggregated information with All For Good partners or other All For Good users. We will not share personal information with any third parties without your consent, except under the narrow circumstances as described above or in the Google Privacy Policy.

Your Choices

  • You do not need to log into a third party Social Service to use the All For Good Site or gadget. You may use All For Good without providing a user name or any other personal information.
  • You can choose to opt out of third party partner access to your All for Good personal information at the time of collection.
  • You can choose to delete the Application's numeric ID and corresponding data from Google's servers by using this form.
  • You can delete cookies stored on your computer by following the instructions for deleting cookies in your browser.

More Information
